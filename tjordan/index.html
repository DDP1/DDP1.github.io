History of Maroon 5

Maroon 5 is a pop rock band that orginated in Los Angeles, California at first they were a highschool band formally called Kara's Flowers as a 4 member band. Kara's flowers signed into Reprise Records and released an album, The Fourth World after a bad start on their album they decided to part ways. After 4 years the band regroup with James Valentine, under the name Maroon 5.

Kara's Flowers first album cover

Conclusion

Maroon 5 being back as a group has now signed with Octone Records and released their debut album in june 2002 called Songs about Jane, The album included 29 songs 3 of which secured top 100 in the Billboard 100 charts. Maroon 5 has recieved several awards since thier debut including 3 Grammy Awards, 3 Billboard Music Awards, and 3 MTV Video Music Awards.
