All About Me

I am the youngest child with two older brothers. I live with my mom and have 2 cats. One of my brothers live with my dad, who has 2 cats, 2 dogs, and 2 one month old puppies. My oldest brother lives on his own.

I went to Cherrypark Elementary school in the David Douglas School District. I moved just before starting at Sellwood Middle School and stayed in PPS at Franklin High School.

I have played the flute, piccolo, and occationally tenor saxaphone since the 5th grade and am currently a proud member of the Franklin High School Symphonic Band Program.

My number 1 hobby is writing books and poetry. I don't watch TV but sometimes enjoy playing videogames. When I was younger, my hobbies were playing basketball, volleyball, and baseball.

After graduating high school, I am at least planning on going to PCC for two years while having a job. I am also planning on publishing at least one of my books around this time.

The White Wolf and The Man in the Mirror

Long ago, there was a prince that fell ill. His younger brother believed in the tale of the Grateful Wolf, which he went to search for. The brother found a white wolf, who feared a hunter and agreed to help the prince for protection. The wolf promised to heal the prince, if they promised to save him from the hunter. After the brother and the prince agreed, the wolf tricked them by stealing the prince's body and the prince turned into the wolf. The Prince-wolf had no choice but to run away from his home in fear of the hunter. When he found a wizard that could help him, he was tricked again by getting a curse that bound him inside a mirror for eturnity.

After a few thousand years, a girl appeared with a way to break the curse. Will she be able to save him?
