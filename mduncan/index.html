Why I chose Computer Coding

I knew Mr.Jamieson would be teaching the class, and I had really enjoyed being a student of his. He helped me a lot and whenever I was stumped and needed help, he was there. He was and is a very helpful and knowledgeable teacher. I had past experience in programming, about three years worth in Javascript and C#. I had programmed inside of Unity 3 and iterations afterward, and just recently Unreal Engine 4. During the summer in 2014 I joined the FHS App developers, I was learnign and going every other weekend to meetings before I had to stop going. During that time I learned HTML, CSS, PHP, and some Ruby due to courses I took at Codeacademy. I am currently developing the game Wrath Of The Goliaths: Dinosaurs, with my team at Ascendence Studios.
