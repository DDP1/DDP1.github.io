About Me

Hi my name is Alex, I am 15 years old and this is my website. I found Youtube back in 2009 and when i got on, I fell in love with the site. I loved watching all of the people on Youtube play all of these different types of video games. In Fact, if I never watched Youtube I would have never found any of the games I play today. I started uploading Youtube videos a couple of years later and since then I have started uploading higher quality videos, and have started live streaming. If you want more information about my Youtube and Twitch channels then read the passages below.
